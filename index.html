--- title: " " author_profile: false layout: home-rbw header: image: /assets/images/HM_landing_page_small.png --- The Head-Marsden Group seeks to elucidate electronic structure properties and open quantum system dynamics relevant in emerging quantum materials and technologies. Our research emphasizes classical method development, alongside algorithm development for use on noisy-intermediate scale quantum computers. With these methods and algorithms, we aim to make predictions for correlated molecular systems undergoing complex environmental interactions with applications in chemistry, physics, and materials science.
