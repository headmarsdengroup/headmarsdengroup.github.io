Graduate students Koray Aydoğan, Tim Krogmeier, and Mikayla Fahrenbruch presented posters at the 
[2026 Research Computing Exhibition](https://msi.umn.edu/events/2026-research-computing-exhibition) 
here at the University of Minnesota! 

 <img src="/assets/images/2026-MSI-Aydogan.jpg" alt="Koray MSI poster“>

Tim’s poster, based on his recent [paper published in the Journal of Physical Chemistry Letters](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/full/10.1021/acs.jpclett.6c00125), was one of seven finalists in the “Physical Sciences and Engineering” category! 

 <img src="/assets/images/2026-MSI-Krogmeier" alt="Tim MSI poster“>

Mikayla’s poster, based on her recent [paper published in the Journal of Physical Chemistry](https://pubs.aip.org/aip/jcp/article/164/6/064108/3379016/Preserving-fermionic-statistics-for-single), was selected to receive a Staff Pick award!

 <img src="/assets/images/2026-MSI-Fahrenbruch" alt="Mikayla MSI poster“>

Congratulations to Koray, Tim, and Mikayla!
