Graduate student Tim Krogmeier was awarded the Doctoral Dissertation Fellowship (DDF) from the University of Minnesota Graduate School 
for the 2026-2027 academic year! The DDF gives the University's most accomplished Ph.D. candidates an opportunity to devote full-time 
effort to an outstanding research project by providing time to write and finalize their dissertation during the fellowship year.

Congratulations Tim!
